Crossword Puzzles Visualizations

Paper: Evaluating Human-Language Model Interaction | Raw Data & Code | Contact: megha@cs.stanford.edu


We provide both static and replay visualizations of users querying 4 different language models (LMs) to solve one of 5 different crossword puzzles. We also report the final puzzle accuracy for each interaction trace (measured per letter). You can use the search bar to search for a particular username, sort by columns, and click on the Open and Play buttons to open the visualization or replay for any particular interaction trace in your browser .
